Return & Exchange Policy

30-Day Return Window

You may return any unused frames in their original condition within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

Eligible Items for Return

  • Non-prescription frames in original packaging
  • Unopened accessories and cases
  • Products with manufacturing defects
  • Damaged items received
  • Incorrect items shipped

Non-Returnable Items

  • Prescription lenses (unless defective)
  • Custom-made or personalized items
  • Gift cards and promotional items
  • Final sale or clearance items
  • Items without original packaging

How to Return

Follow these simple steps to return your order:

  1. Log into your account at frames.com
  2. Navigate to "Order History" and select your order
  3. Click "Request Return" and select items to return
  4. Choose your reason for return
  5. Print the prepaid return label we email you
  6. Package items securely in their original packaging
  7. Attach the return label to your package
  8. Drop off at any authorized shipping location

Refund Process

Once we receive your return:

  1. Inspection completed within 2 business days
  2. Refund processed within 5-7 business days
  3. Refund issued to original payment method
  4. Email confirmation sent when refund is processed

Note: It may take an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account depending on your bank.

Exchanges

Warranty Information

1-Year Limited Warranty

All frames products come with a 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. This warranty begins from the date of purchase and applies to the original purchaser only.

What's Covered

Our warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship, including:

  • Frame breakage due to manufacturing defects
  • Lens coating defects (peeling, bubbling, cracking)
  • Hinge failures or hardware issues
  • Defective prescription lenses
  • Bridge or temple separation
  • Paint or finish defects

What's Not Covered

The warranty does not cover:

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Accidental damage or breakage
  • Lost or stolen items
  • Scratches from improper cleaning or use
  • Damage from unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Damage from extreme temperatures or chemicals
  • Lens prescription changes
  • Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect functionality

Making a Warranty Claim

To file a warranty claim:

  1. Contact our customer service team via email or phone
  2. Provide your order number and purchase date
  3. Submit clear photos showing the defect
  4. Describe the issue in detail
  5. Our team will review within 1-2 business days
  6. If approved, we'll provide a prepaid shipping label
  7. Send the item to our warranty center
  8. Receive a replacement or repair within 7-10 business days

Extended Warranty Options

Protect your frames beyond the standard warranty:

  • 2-Year Extended Warranty: $29.99/₹3000 - Covers all manufacturing defects
  • 3-Year Complete Protection: $49.99/₹5000 - Includes accidental damage coverage
  • Lifetime Protection Plan: $89.99/₹9000 - Full coverage for the life of your frames

Extended warranties can be purchased within 30 days of your original order.

Warranty Limitations

  • Warranty is non-transferable and applies only to the original purchaser
  • Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims
  • We reserve the right to repair or replace defective items at our discretion
  • Warranty does not cover consequential or incidental damages
  • International orders may have different warranty terms

Care Instructions

To maintain your warranty coverage, please follow these care guidelines:

  1. Clean lenses with provided microfiber cloth only
  2. Store frames in protective case when not in use
  3. Avoid exposure to extreme heat or cold
  4. Do not use harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners
  5. Have frames adjusted by professionals only
  6. Keep frames away from water and moisture when possible
